About the Label

Hard ground.
Uncompromising.

Caliche Records was built on a simple belief — the most powerful music comes from somewhere real, and you can hear it when it's not.

We work with music that doesn't ask permission. That doesn't fit neatly into formats. That sits in the shadows between tradition and something harder, darker, entirely its own.

No shortcuts. No compromises. Just songs.

  • Music that has something to say
  • Built to last, not to trend
  • Rooted deep, built on our terms
  • Independent. Always.
