About the Label

Hard ground.
Honest music.

Caliche Records was built on a simple belief — the most powerful music comes from people who have lived something worth singing about.

We work with artists who don't ask permission. Who don't fit neatly into formats. Who make music that sits in the dark corners of American roots, outlaw country, and the wide restless space in between.

No shortcuts. No compromises. Just songs.

  • Artists who have something to say
  • Music built to last, not to trend
  • Rooted in American soil, built outside the machine
  • Independent. Always.
